About Us

Bryan's Café is a family-run café located across Sydney's most vibrant neighbourhoods. We believe great coffee and honest food make every day a little better.

Our menu celebrates seasonal, locally sourced produce. Every dish is crafted by our talented kitchen team with care and passion. Whether you are grabbing a quick espresso or settling in for a long lunch, you will always feel at home at Bryan's.

We are committed to sustainability, using recyclable packaging and reducing food waste wherever possible. Friendly service, cosy spaces, and outstanding flavours — that is the Bryan's Café promise.

Our History

Bryan's Café opened its doors in Surry Hills in 2005 with a single espresso machine and a dream.

Founded by Bryan O'Sullivan, a passionate barista with years of experience in Melbourne's café scene, the original Surry Hills location quickly became a neighbourhood institution. Long queues, loyal regulars, and word-of-mouth praise led to expansion.

By 2012, Bryan's Café had opened its second location in Haymarket, followed by the Circular Quay branch in 2017. Today, with three thriving locations and a growing team, Bryan's Café continues to serve Sydney with the same warmth and quality that started it all.
